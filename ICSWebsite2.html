The Basics

To get better at the game, you have to master the basics first. To move use the left stick and press it down to sprint. The right allows you to look around and pressing it down activates a melee action. To aim down the sights of the weapon (ads), press the left trigger. The right trigger allows you to shoot from the gun. To throw tactical and lethal grenades, press down the top left trigger, and the top right trigger. On the right side of the controller, the triangle button switches the weapon you are using, the square button reloads your weapon, the "x" button allows you your character to jump and mantle over objects, and finally the circle button has you crouch but if you hold it down you can go prone. The tab button of the controller brings up the scoreboard showing how well (or poor) you, and your teammates are performing in games.
