Weapons

There are 8 main weapon sections. There's the Sub-Machine Gun section (SMGs), Assault Rifles (ARs), Shotguns, Light Machine Guns (LMGs), Sniper Rifles, Pistols, Rocket Launchers, and finally the Knife. There is also occasionally a riot shield which would change 8 sections to 9.
